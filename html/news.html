Welcome to NGP

By Daniel Suh

Welcome to the launch of NextGenPrinting, a small 3D printing and CAD service-oriented company looking to support students, artists, and small companies with our services. For the longest time, we were talking about forming a start-up such as this one. Finally, our dream has become a reality. From all of us at NGP, we would like to extend thanks to our friends and families for supporting us thus far. Explore the website and follow us on Facebook as our company grows so that we may better strengthen our community and help you make your dreams into realities. If you have any questions for us, please email us at NGP@thinkbeyondink.net. Thank you all again for your support!

-Think Beyond Ink!
